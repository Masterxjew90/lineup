Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

This page explains what information Lineup collects, how it's used, and the rules for using the service. Lineup is a free tool that helps small live streamers get discovered and support one another.

Who we are

Lineup ("Lineup," "we," "us") is an independent, free service for discovering and supporting small live streamers. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Twitch, Meta/Facebook, or any other platform. Using Lineup is optional and free.

What information we collect

Lineup only collects what it needs to work. Specifically:

From your Twitch account (required to sign in)

  • Your Twitch username, display name, profile picture, and account ID.
  • With your permission, your list of followed channels and your current live status — used to show you who you follow that's live, to verify a follow when you earn discovery points, and to feature you when you go live.

Activity you create on Lineup

  • Your points and point history (watching, feedback, discovery, collabs, raids) and your standing on the leaderboard.
  • Your lineup status (whether you've listed yourself) and any featured time you've bought with points.
  • Content you add to your profile page: your bio, accent color, social links, background image link, music link, section layout, and wall posts.
  • Feedback messages you send to other streamers.

If you choose to connect Facebook (optional)

  • Whether your Facebook is verified, and your Facebook display name, only to show a "verified" badge on your profile. Nothing is posted to Facebook, and we do not access your friends, photos, or posts.

How we use your information

  • To sign you in and keep you signed in.
  • To show live streamers, power discovery, and run the points and leaderboard system.
  • To display your public profile page to others.
  • To verify genuine actions (like confirming a follow actually happened) so points reflect real engagement.

We do not sell your information, show third-party ads, or use your data for advertising profiles.

Facebook connection

Connecting Facebook is entirely optional and exists for one purpose: to put a verified badge on your profile proving the Facebook account is really yours. We confirm the link through Facebook's login and store only the fact that you're verified plus your Facebook display name. You can use every feature of Lineup without ever connecting Facebook, and adding a Facebook link to your profile does not require connecting it.

What is public

Lineup is a community discovery tool, so some things are visible to everyone:

  • Your profile page — your name, picture, bio, links, points, wall posts, and any verified badge — is public and shareable by link.
  • Your position on the leaderboard and your presence in the lineup when you list yourself.

Your Twitch access token and email (if any) are never shown publicly. Don't post anything on your wall or profile you wouldn't want to be public.

Third-party services

Lineup relies on a few services to function. Your use of them is governed by their own policies:

  • Twitch — for login and live stream data. (Twitch's Privacy Notice and Terms apply to your Twitch account.)
  • Meta / Facebook — only if you choose to connect it, for identity verification.
  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider, which stores your account and Lineup activity.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the Lineup website.
  • Profile music and background links you add may load content from services like YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud when someone views your page.

Storage & cookies

Lineup stores your sign-in session and a few small items (such as your active watch timer) in your browser's local storage so the site works smoothly. Your account data is stored in our Supabase database. We don't use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers.

Age requirement & mature content

You must be at least 13 years old to use Lineup, in line with Twitch's own minimum age. The first time you sign in, we ask your date of birth to determine whether you're 18 or older; we store only that yes/no result, never your birthdate.

Streams marked mature by the streamer or by Twitch, and certain age-restricted categories, are hidden by default and only shown to users confirmed to be 18+. This is a standard first-layer filter that relies on streamers labeling their content honestly — it reduces, but cannot perfectly guarantee, that no mature content is ever shown.

Terms of use

By using Lineup, you agree to these terms. If you don't agree, please don't use the service.

  • Use Lineup lawfully and respectfully. Don't harass, threaten, impersonate, or abuse other people.
  • Don't try to cheat the points system, exploit bugs, automate actions, or interfere with how the service works.
  • Don't use Lineup to gain followers or views through deception, bots, or any method that breaks Twitch's or another platform's rules. Lineup is built to reward genuine engagement.
  • Lineup is provided "as is," free of charge, with no warranty. We can't guarantee it will always be available, accurate, or error-free.
  • We may change, suspend, or discontinue any part of Lineup at any time.

Content you post

You are responsible for everything you add to Lineup — your bio, links, background image, music, and wall posts. By posting it, you confirm you have the right to share it and that it doesn't break the law or these terms.

Do not post or link to content that is illegal, hateful, harassing, sexually explicit, that sexualizes minors, that infringes someone's copyright, or that points to malware or scams. Because backgrounds and links are content you provide, they are your responsibility.

We may remove any content, badge, or account — and may restrict or end your access — if we believe it breaks these terms or harms other users, with or without notice.

Accessing or deleting your data

You can clear most of your profile content yourself by editing your page. To delete your account and associated data, or to ask what data we hold about you, email us using the contact below and we'll take care of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page as Lineup grows or as requirements change. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continuing to use Lineup after a change means you accept the updated version.
